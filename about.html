About Us

R.R.P. RAJ INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED is a professionally managed industrial and infrastructure services company established in 2020. Formerly known as R.R.P. RAJ, we have built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, safe, and high-precision engineering solutions across industrial, commercial, residential, and infrastructure projects.

We specialize in concrete cutting, drilling, anchoring, grouting, civil construction, mechanical works, waterproofing, and manpower and machinery support. Our services are executed by a skilled workforce using advanced tools and modern machinery, ensuring accuracy, durability, and timely completion of every project.

Under the leadership of Mr. Abhishek Dayal Raj and Mr. Abhijit Dayal Raj, the company has achieved steady growth and earned the trust of leading organizations across India and abroad. Our client-focused approach, strict quality control, and commitment to safety enable us to deliver consistent and dependable results.

Beyond business, we are committed to responsible growth through ethical practices and social responsibility initiatives, supporting skill development and employment opportunities for young and underprivileged individuals.
